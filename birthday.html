60th Birthday Card Ideas — “10 of…” prompts

Plan: 6 siblings, each sends 1 card with a “10 of…” list (6 × 10 = 60). Pick six categories from the list below and divvy them up.

About the recipient

  • 10 specific memories of them
  • 10 stories about them you’ve told other people
  • 10 times they made you laugh
  • 10 times they bailed you out
  • 10 of their catchphrases / things they always say
  • 10 things they taught you (directly or by example)
  • 10 things you’ve learned watching them

Questions

  • 10 questions you’ve always wanted to ask them
  • 10 “what would you do if…” hypotheticals
  • 10 questions to answer at the next family gathering

Year of birth (1966, if turning 60 in 2026)

  • 10 news events from 1966 (Miranda ruling, Star Trek premiere, England wins the World Cup, Medicare begins paying, etc.)
  • 10 #1 songs from 1966
  • 10 movies released in 1966
  • 10 prices of everyday things in 1966
  • 10 people born the same year
  • 10 things that didn’t exist yet in 1966

Decade-by-decade (60 = 6 decades)

  • 10 things from the decade they were born / grew up / came of age in
  • 10 songs that mark each decade of their life
  • 10 photos spanning the six decades
  • 10 cultural moments they lived through

Recommendations / a “gift” inside the card

  • 10 song recommendations (a playlist or mix CD)
  • 10 book recommendations
  • 10 movie or TV recommendations
  • 10 podcast recommendations
  • 10 recipes (handwritten family ones, or new ones to try)
  • 10 restaurants in their town worth hitting
  • 10 day trips within driving distance
  • 10 small pleasures to try this decade

Forward-looking

  • 10 predictions for their 60s
  • 10 wishes for the next decade
  • 10 dares / challenges for the next 10 years
  • 10 bucket-list suggestions
  • 10 places to visit before 70
  • 10 skills to pick up
  • 10 things to say yes to
  • 10 things to say no to

Sensory / specific

  • 10 smells that remind you of them
  • 10 sounds from your shared past
  • 10 photos of objects that remind you of them
  • 10 places (with addresses) tied to your shared history
  • 10 songs to play in the car alone

Light / funny

  • 10 jokes (the worse the better)
  • 10 puns at their expense
  • 10 ridiculous fake predictions
  • 10 fake horoscopes for the year
  • 10 lies about them that sound true
  • 10 truths about them that sound like lies

Reflective

  • 10 quotes (from books/songs/people) that make you think of them
  • 10 reminders of who they are when they forget
