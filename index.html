About

Rooted in the
American Tradition

Peter Keane is a guitarist and vocalist based in Austin, TX. He plays traditional American music — country blues, folk, gospel, and old-time — drawing primarily from the repertoire of artists like Mississippi John Hurt, Doc Watson, and the Carter Family.

He has an active YouTube channel with over a thousand videos, including live performances and instructional videos covering fingerpicking techniques, song tutorials, and gear.
